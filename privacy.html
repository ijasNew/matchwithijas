Match with Ijas Privacy Policy

Last updated: 13 December 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Match with Ijas collects, uses and protects personal information for our personalized, religion‑neutral matchmaking services for educated professionals and families in Malappuram, Calicut and the Malabar region.

1. Information We Collect

Depending on how you use our services, we may collect:

  • Basic details: name, age, gender, city, education and profession.
  • Contact details: phone number, WhatsApp number and email address.
  • Matchmaking preferences: partner expectations, family requirements and other criteria.
  • Communication history: messages, notes and feedback related to suggested matches.

2. How We Use Your Matchmaking Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide personalized marriage consultancy and matchmaking in Malappuram and Calicut.
  • Share suitable profiles with you and check mutual interest with other families.
  • Arrange and coordinate meetings or calls when both parties are interested.
  • Improve our services, internal reporting and marketing in an aggregated or anonymised form.

3. Data Security & Confidentiality

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to keep your data safe and reduce the risk of loss, misuse or unauthorised access. Only authorised team members involved in matchmaking can access your personal information.

We do not sell your data or share it with random individuals or unrelated businesses. Profile details are shared only with relevant prospects for genuine alliance discussions.

4. Sharing of Information

Your information may be shared in these limited situations:

  • With shortlisted matches and families, after screening, for genuine marriage proposals.
  • With service providers who support our operations (for example communication tools), under confidentiality obligations.
  • With authorities if required by law or to protect our legal rights and safety.

5. Marketing & Advertising Use

We may use non‑sensitive or aggregated information (such as age range, profession, city or service type) to understand our audience and run our own marketing or advertising campaigns.

We do not publicly display your full name, contact number or private details in advertisements without your explicit consent.

6. Data Retention

We keep your information for as long as it is reasonably needed to provide matchmaking and follow‑up services or as required by law. You can request that we stop using your profile for new matches at any time.

7. Your Choices & Rights

You can:

  • Ask us to update or correct your information if it is incomplete or inaccurate.
  • Request that we stop contacting you for new matches or marketing messages.
  • Request deletion of your details from our active records, subject to legal or contractual limits.

8. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our services or legal requirements. The latest version will always be available on this page, and the “Last updated” date will show the current version.
